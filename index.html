About the Artist

Pooja Mehta paints from a place where emotion meets stillness. Her work explores the unseen — the quiet stories within color, light, and texture. Each piece is a meditation, inviting the viewer to pause and feel.

With a Fine Arts background and exhibitions across India, Pooja’s art continues to find homes in collections both local and international.
