Artist's Statement

Puja Mehta is a vicenarian who believes in expressing rather than illustrating. She is enthusiastic, overwhelmed, and grateful for the art she is doing. Being a mural painter who works primarily in acrylic paints, she has also dabbled with oil paints.

She qualified in this indefinable field from the Kanoria Centre for Arts, Ahmedabad, and has experienced herself for 4 years till now, after qualifying post-graduation in commerce from a renowned university.

She gets inspired by people’s perspectives, which enhances her ability to express herself. The purpose of the eye, which is the main element of her artworks, is to get the viewer's intent within the artworks and present it into reality through creative fingers. She is keen to learn about further diversification in painting.
